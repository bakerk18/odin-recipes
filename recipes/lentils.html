Simple Lentils

Description

This simple and filling dish is ready in minutes! Lentils are high in protein and super affordable.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups dry brown lentils
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 3 cups boiling water, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • cilantro, chopped (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Pour lentils into a large mixing bowl, and rinse and drain. Pour in some boiling water on top, enough to cover the lentils, and let it sit for 10 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large pot or 4 quart Dutch oven, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and turmeric. Saut√© until onions are soft and translucent, about 2-3 minutes. Pour in 1 cup of boiling water into the pot and stir together.
  3. Drain the lentils that have been soaking in water, then rinse and drain again. Stir the lentils into the pot along with the remaining boiling water, cumin, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Turn the heat up to high and bring to a boil. Then, turn to low and simmer covered for 15 minutes.
  4. Season with more salt and pepper to taste. Add cilantro and serve.
